About the Paradox Trello

The official Paradox Roblox Trello is maintained by the developer "a bleach game" at trello.com/b/IkzpkDRy/paradox-information-new. It contains 345 cards across 26 lists — every faction description, clan roll table, power rarity distribution, skill tree, boss drop, NPC role, and update note. It is the single source of truth for Paradox mechanics.

The official board has two problems. First, Trello's mobile rendering is slow: on a mid-range phone over 4G the full 345-card board takes 8-12 seconds to become interactive. Second, Trello has no cross-list search — if you want to know which items drop from Starrk, or which talents are Speed-exclusive, you have to open every card manually. This mirror solves both: the entire board is inlined as static HTML (loads in under 500ms on any connection), and the search box at the top filters all cards instantly.

We re-sync from the live Trello every six hours. Patch-note cards and new items usually appear here within the same day the developer posts them. We do not edit, summarize, or reinterpret the official data — this is a faithful mirror, not an opinionated wiki. If you want rankings and opinions, the tier list is the right page; if you want to build around the data, the build planner uses these same clan stats and power rarities.

A note on completeness: the Accessories section above shows only the top Legendary/Mythical pieces. The full Trello has 63 accessory cards across 7 slots (Torso, Head, Neck, Face, Back, Arm, Waist). Use the search box to find any specific item. If you notice a card is missing or outdated, the Discord link at the top of the page leads to our feedback channel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the official Paradox Trello?

At trello.com/b/IkzpkDRy/paradox-information-new. It is publicly viewable and maintained by the developer.

Why use this mirror instead of the official board?

Speed and search. The official board takes 8-12 seconds to load on mobile and has no cross-list search. This mirror loads under 500ms and supports instant keyword filtering across all 345 cards.

How often is the mirror updated?

Every six hours. New patch cards usually appear within the same day as the Discord announcement.

Where can I see every accessory / item / NPC?

Use the search box above — it filters across all 345 cards regardless of section. Typing "accessory" or "npc" is not useful since those are internal labels; instead search the item name directly (e.g., "diamond", "skull", "cape").
