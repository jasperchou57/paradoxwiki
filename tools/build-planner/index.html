How the Paradox Build Planner Works

Every clan stat buff in this planner comes directly from the official Paradox Trello's Soul Reaper Clans, Hollow Clans, and Quincy Clans cards. For example, Aizen's buff "+10 HP, +10 Reiatsu, +5 Posture" is the exact text from the Trello — we do not add, average, or estimate. Common clans (Akagi, Vega, Accutrone, etc.) show zero buffs because the Trello explicitly states "Common Clans (No Buffs)" — if you roll one, consider a Clan Reroll.

Power rarity reflects the Trello drop-rate tiers published on the Soul-reaper Powers, Arrancar Powers, and Quincy Powers cards: Legendary powers drop at 1% per roll, Rare at 10%, Common at 89%. The planner does not currently model combat damage because the Trello does not publish raw damage formulas — powers are differentiated only by their tier. If the developer publishes per-power damage values in a future update, we will add a DPS column within 24 hours.

The Build Score compares your stat totals against the theoretical maximum for your race. A 100% build means you picked the stat-leader Legendary clan for your race and the highest-tier power available. Below 100% the planner surfaces specific swap suggestions that would raise your score the most — usually a single Clan Reroll matters more than several stat tree changes.

The Potential slider reflects the real progression gates published on the Trello: at 75 Potential you can take the Soul Reaper Exam, at 200 you unlock Shikai (by pressing N), at 215 Quincies can begin the Schrift quest, at 605 Hollows can start the Resurrection quest, and at 705 you cap out. The slider's current state shows which gate you've passed — useful for confirming whether a build is actually reachable at your progression.

Unlike opinion-based tier lists, this planner uses only the developer's published data. If the Trello changes, the planner changes. If you see a discrepancy between the planner and the Trello, the Trello wins — please report it via the Discord link at the bottom of the page.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Paradox Build Planner do?

It lets you pick race, clan, power, and stat investment and shows HP, Reiatsu, and Posture totals from real Trello data. You can share the build via URL.

Is it free? Do I need to sign up?

Free, no signup. There are no ads on the planner and no email capture. Build state is stored locally in your browser.

Where do the numbers come from?

Directly from the official Paradox Trello — Soul Reaper Clans, Hollow Clans, Quincy Clans, and the three Power cards. We do not estimate.

Why isn't there a DPS number?

The Trello does not publish raw damage formulas — only clan stat buffs and power rarities. We will not invent numbers. When the developer publishes damage values, we will add a DPS column within 24 hours.

Can I share a build with my friends?

Yes. Click Share build. The URL encodes your complete loadout. Paste it anywhere (Discord, Reddit, YouTube descriptions) and the recipient sees the same build.
