How the Tier List Works

This tier list is computed from the raw stat buffs listed on the official Paradox Trello. Clans are ranked by the sum of their HP, Reiatsu, and Posture bonuses, weighted equally. Powers are ranked by their rarity (Legendary > Rare > Common) because the developer has explicitly tied drop rate to power tier — the 1% roll is there precisely because those powers are better.

Races are harder to rank because they do not share a comparable stat. Quincy wins the race tier because it is the only race with a flat damage reduction passive (Blut Stats: 15%), which applies to every encounter regardless of build. Soul Reaper and Hollow are tied in A-tier: Soul Reapers reach their first power tier fastest (Shikai at 200 Potential vs Hollow needing to grind through 4 evolution stages), while Hollows have a longer late-game ceiling (Resurrection unlocks at 605 Potential versus Bankai's progression path).

The Legendary power ranking is flat within Legendary — the Trello does not yet specify which Legendary power is stronger, so Rampage, Flower, Gravity (Soul Reaper), Gunners, Bat, Divinity (Arrancar), and Fear, Deathdealing (Quincy) are all ranked S. If the community produces consistent data that one Legendary outperforms others, we will rebuild this section.

When the developer updates clan stats, power rarities, or boss drops on the Trello, we update this page within 24 hours. The Trello is the source of truth — if a clan's buffs change on the Trello but not here, the Trello wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best clan in Paradox Roblox right now?

By total stat points: Louisenbairn (Hollow, 29) and Nkk Le Vaar (Quincy, 35 weighted). By balance and flexibility: Aizen (Soul Reaper) and Cifer (Hollow). By raw HP: Yhwach (Quincy, +20 HP). Pick based on the build you want to run.

Is this tier list official?

No. It is computed from the official Trello's published stat values. The developer does not publish their own tier list. If they release one, we will cite it.

How often is the tier list updated?

Within 24 hours of any balance change on the official Trello.

What race should I pick as a new player?

Soul Reaper if you want the fastest path to a flashy power (Shikai at 200 Potential), Quincy if you want the highest survivability from day one (Blut 15% damage reduction), Hollow if you enjoy a longer, more involved progression (Fishbone → Vastocar → Arrancar → Resurrection).

How many Ability Rerolls do I need to hit a Legendary power?

At 1% drop rate, expected rolls for 50% probability is 69, for 63% is 100, and for 95% is 299. The launch-week codes paradoxisout, Iblamethetesters, and morebugfixes combined give you 70+ Ability Rerolls — enough for roughly a coin-flip at a Legendary.
