About Paradox Roblox Wiki

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Paradox Wiki (paradoxwiki.net) is an independent community resource for the Paradox Roblox Bleach-inspired action RPG, released by the developer "a bleach game" on April 10, 2026. We exist to give Paradox players faster, cleaner access to the real game data than the official Trello and Discord alone can provide on a phone.

What We Cover

  • Live Codes — every working code, synced hourly from the official Discord and Trello. Current count: 15 active, 0 expired.
  • Trello Mirror — all 345 cards from the official Paradox Trello, loadable in under 500ms with cross-list search.
  • Clan Tier List — every Legendary, Rare, and Common clan for Soul Reaper, Hollow, and Quincy, ranked by real HP/Reiatsu/Posture buffs.
  • Build Planner — free tool for choosing race, clan, power, and stats. Shareable URLs, no signup, localStorage auto-save.
  • Progression Guides — how to wipe, Shikai unlock, Resurrection questline, Schrift obtainment.

Why This Site Exists

The official Paradox Trello is the source of truth, but Trello's mobile rendering is slow and has no cross-list search. Roblox players play on phones. That mismatch leaves a gap — fast, mobile-first, searchable Paradox information — that was not filled by any existing site at launch. Fandom has no active Paradox wiki. Content-farm guide sites publish shallow listicles that repeat the same five codes across 20 domains. We decided to build the thing we wanted to use.

Every page is a single static HTML file. The entire site weighs under 1 MB including the 15 WebP images and the Build Planner's real clan data. There is no login wall, no signup popup, no newsletter modal, and no full-screen ad. We use Google Analytics to decide which guides to improve next — nothing more.

Data Sources

Everything on this site is sourced from publicly available Paradox data:

  • Official Trello — trello.com/b/IkzpkDRy. The board has 345 cards across 26 lists, maintained by the developer. We re-sync every six hours.
  • Official Discord — discord.gg/ableachgame. 166,000+ members. Codes are pinned in the #announcements channel.
  • Community testing — build performance observations shared in Discord and on YouTube. We attribute these where relevant.
  • YouTube community videos — we link to verified Paradox Bleach guides from MimoBlox, Catt, Breazsy, LiftedTIO, HW5567, and others. We do not embed iframes; we link to the video with a thumbnail to keep page weight low.

We do not invent numbers. If the Trello does not publish a statistic (for example, raw damage formulas), we do not fabricate one and we do not display a placeholder dressed up as real data. The Build Planner uses only stat buffs that are explicitly in the Trello, which is why we show HP / Reiatsu / Posture totals instead of a fake "DPS" number.

How We Handle Corrections

When the developer updates clan stats, power rarities, code lists, or progression thresholds on the Trello, we update this site within 24 hours. The fastest way to report an error or suggest a correction is to reach us in the official Paradox Discord feedback channel — reference the exact page URL and we will investigate within the day.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell user data, and we have none to sell (see Privacy Policy).
  • We do not run targeted advertising. The site currently has no ads at all.
  • We do not accept sponsored tier list rankings. Rankings are based on published Trello stats.
  • We do not mirror paywalled or leaked content. If it is not on the public Trello or Discord, it is not here.
  • We do not offer Robux, rerolls, or in-game items for sale. The free code list on /codes/ is the full story.
  • We do not claim any affiliation with "a bleach game", Roblox Corporation, or Tite Kubo. See the Terms for the full disclaimer.

Contact and Credits

Paradox Wiki is a volunteer community project. There is no parent company, no LLC, and no full-time staff. For corrections, DMCA notices, or collaboration requests, the official Paradox Discord feedback channel is the single contact point — we read it daily.
